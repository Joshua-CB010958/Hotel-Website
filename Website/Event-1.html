Misogi retreat

Find satori (enlightenment) with this three-night, full-board retreat named for the Shinto purification ritual. Combining bodywork, nutrition and Japanese purification practices, it includes Kodo Zen incense meditation, tea ceremonies, sword training and more. With its 2,500-square-metre Aman Spa and 30-metre pool, Aman Tokyo is the ideal urban sanctuary in which to complete such a journey.
